Haunted House

::Beginnings
You are in a small, dimly lit room. There is a door in front of you. You hear a faint voice coming from the other side of the door. It says, "I am the ghost of the house. I will grant you one wish. What is your wish?"
[Try to open the door](Another room)

::Another room
You find yourself in a larger room, filled with dusty furniture and cobwebs. There's an old, worn-out book lying on a table in the corner of the room.
[Open the book](The book of spells)
[Go back](Beginnings)

::The book of spells
As you open the book, you discover it's full of ancient spells and enchantments. You can feel the power emanating from the pages.
[Read a random spell](Random spell)
[Close the book and go back](Another room)

::Random spell
You read a random spell out loud, and suddenly the room is filled with bright light! When the light fades, you find yourself back in the small, dimly lit room where you started.
[Explore the house more](More exploration)
[Leave the house](Leave)

::More exploration
You decide to explore the house further and find a hidden door that leads to a secret chamber. Inside, you discover a treasure chest!
[Open the treasure chest](Treasure chest)
[Go back to the small, dimly lit room](Beginnings)

::Treasure chest
You open the treasure chest, and inside you find a pile of gold coins and precious gems. You've found the hidden treasure of the haunted house!
[Take the treasure and leave the house](Leave)
[Keep exploring](More exploration)

::Leave
Having seen enough of the haunted house, you decide to leave. You exit the house and never look back.
